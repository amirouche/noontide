babelia
babelia is a privacy friendly, decentralized, open source, and accessible search engine.
Search has been an essential part of knowledge acquisition from the dawn of time, whether it is antic lexicographic ordered filing cabinets or nowadays computer-based wonders such as Google or Bing. From casual search to help achieve common tasks such as cooking, keeping up with the news, regular dose of cat memes or professional search such as science research. Search is, and will remain an essential daily-use tool, and stires human progress forward.
babelia aims to replace the use of privateer search engine with a search engine that is open, hence under the control of the common.
Eventually, babelia may offer features commonly associated with existing search engines such as maps, mail accounts, calendar, image and video gallery, feed reader inside a single coherent code base. babelia wants to be an easy to install, easy to use, easy to maintain, no-code, personal search engine that can scale to billions of documents, beyond the terabyte of text data, under €100 a month per babelia instance.
